Xiaodong Jia’s Portfolio

Xiaodong Jia “Ancient future” (May 2019) Experimental Music

This work is a piece of ambient music I made after I graduated from Musicians Institute. I want to represent the strange but beautiful feelings, like in ancient times with a world of mysterious creatures. Also, giving people the time travel feeling. The record mainly focuses on the modular synth, and I arranged and edited them in the box. I wrote the chorus for ambient sound first, and then I used the modular synth called Ornament & Crime sequenced the melody. After I created the musical environment, I added other sounds that I made with the granular sampler step by step. I really love making this kind of ambient music because it is a part of who I am.






Xiaodong Jia “Immortal Cascade” (November 2018) Experimental Music

This work is also a piece of ambient music. It mainly describes a dream I had, which is about a blurry scene of a cascade. After I woke up, I immediately wrote the idea down in text and then made it out in a few days. The process is similar to previous work. I mainly made it with modular synthesizers and I did post-production in the box. The Ornament & Crime and the Morphagene from the Make noise were key compositional tools in this process. s






Xiaodong Jia( Video from https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iTf1K-8pL7E on youtube) “Riverbond - Launch Trailer”(November 2019) Sound Design and Music Composition for Moving Image

The first work of moving images is the Riverbond game trailer. I recorded most of the sound in my studio. Then, I experiment with the pitch for the sound that I recorded, and I use a plugin called Redux to add an 8-bit texture. Besides, I used the synthesizer to create some of those FX, weapon and explosion sound. Also, I made the chiptune music for this trailer to reflect the 8-bit texture. I am really interested in this kind of sound design.






Xiaodong Jia( Video from https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MsbL8lFHrZI) “Warframe - Official Cinematic Opening Trailer” (November 2019) Sound Design and Music Composition for Moving Image

The second work for moving images is the game trailer for the Warframe. The Warframe is a game I played recently, so I decided to replace the sound and compose music for its trailer. For the sound design, I layered sounds that I found and recorded to improve the quality and to add texture. Also, I recorded some of those ambient sounds for this trailer. This kind of work is what I am working on recently, and I want to do it as my future career.






Xiaodong Jia( Video from https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p3nAGW051Jw on youtube) “Gungrave: G.O.R.E. - Reunion Trailer” (November 2019) Sound Design and Music Composition for Moving Image

The third work for moving images is the game trailer for the Gore. It is also a game that I’m into, so I redesigned the sound for it. I made the background music with the synthesizer, and I layered audio samples and used those sounds to match the video and reflect the violent and realistic feeling. Like I said, I want to do sound design similar to this kind of work in the future.
